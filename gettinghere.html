HOW TO GET TO MUZIUM NEGARA

    KTM Komuter, LRT, Monorail and ERL

    Alight at KL Sentral station and walk through the connecting station to go to MRT National Museum.

    By MRT

    Take the MRT and head straight to the Muzium Negara Station.

    Parking Area

    Comfortable parking areas are available at the National Museum.
