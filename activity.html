ACTIVITY YOU CAN DO

Explore Permanent Exhibits

  • The museum's permanent galleries showcase Malaysia's history and culture, from prehistoric times to the present day.

Visit Temporary Exhibitions

  • The museum regularly hosts temporary exhibitions that focus on specific themes or topics related to Malaysia's heritage and culture.

Guided Tours

  • Visitors can join guided tours provided by the museum, offering deeper insights into the exhibits and the history they represent.

Cultural Performances

  • The museum occasionally hosts cultural performances, including traditional dance, music, and theater, which provide a lively experience of Malaysia's cultural diversity.

Workshops and Educational Programs

  • These programs can include hands-on activities, crafts, and lectures on various historical and cultural topics.

Museum Shop

  • Visitors can browse the museum shop for souvenirs, books, and other items related to Malaysia's history and culture.

Special Events

  • The museum organizes special events during festivals and national holidays, often featuring additional activities and displays.

Outdoor Exhibits

  • The museum grounds feature outdoor exhibits, including traditional Malaysian houses and historical artifacts, which visitors can explore.

Interactive Displays

  • Some sections of the museum have interactive displays and multimedia presentations that engage visitors in a more immersive learning experience.

Photography Opportunities

  • The museum's architecture and exhibits offer great photography opportunities, allowing visitors to capture memorable moments of their visit.
