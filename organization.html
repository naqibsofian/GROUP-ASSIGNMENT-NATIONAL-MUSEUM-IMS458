OUR ORGANIZATION

The Exhibition and Research Unit:Exhibitions and research are a medium that connects the museum institution with the community and is one of the important vehicles for the National Museum in preserving, collecting and disseminating knowledge
The Education Unit: Educational activities are held with the aim of nurturing and further disseminating the functions and roles of the National Museum as an informal educational institution that upholds heritage, history, nature, and culture.
The Museum Management Unit: The Museum Management Unit is an important support to the main functions of the National Museum to ensure that the policies and objectives of the National Museum can be achieved and implemented efficiently and effectively to all parties, especially the main customers of the National Museum, which are visitors.
